Who is SF12P?

I'm a mother of 1 beautiful girl and love her to absolute death. And I love coding... (we have a love hate relationship) but I'm a developer simply making stuff I wanted to in the moment. My goal isn't to make the next big thing. And it isn't to make something so random it's useless. It's something I wanted or needed but didn't exist yet. I simply make tools for problems other tools were a little too loose for. Yeah they would work. But you would at least be happier if the tool fit a little better — no?
